## RCB Secures Dominant Victory Over CSK in IPL 2026 Thriller

**Bengaluru:** Royal Challengers Bangalore (RCB) defeated Chennai Super Kings (CSK) by 43 runs in a high-scoring encounter at the Chinnaswamy Stadium. RCB posted a mammoth 250/3, the highest-ever total against CSK in IPL history, powered by a blistering unbeaten 70 off 25 balls from Tim David, who also hit a massive 106-meter six. CSK were bowled out for 207 in 19.4 overs.

**Key Developments:**

*   **RCB's Record-Breaking Innings:** RCB set a new IPL record against CSK by scoring 250/3. Contributions included Phil Salt's 46 off 30 balls, Devdutt Padikkal's 50 off 29 balls, Rajat Patidar's unbeaten 48 off 19 balls, and Tim David's explosive 70 not out.
*   **CSK's Bowling and Batting Efforts:** CSK captain Ruturaj Gaikwad won the toss and elected to bowl. Despite picking up wickets, including Virat Kohli (28 off 18 balls) dismissed by Anshul Kamboj, their bowlers could not contain RCB's aggressive batting lineup. In response, Sarfaraz Khan scored a half-century (50 off 25 balls), and contributions came from Jamie Overton (37) and Prashant Veer (43), but it wasn't enough to chase down the large total. CSK were bowled out for 207.
*   **Bowling Milestones:** Bhuvneshwar Kumar reached a significant milestone, taking 3 wickets for 41 runs and becoming the second bowler in TATA IPL history to achieve 200 wickets. Jacob Duffy also took early wickets for RCB.
