# RCB Dominates Delhi Capitals in Nine-Wicket Victory

## Overview
Royal Challengers Bengaluru (RCB) secured a resounding nine-wicket victory over Delhi Capitals (DC) in their TATA IPL 2026 match. After electing to bowl first, RCB's bowlers put on a stellar performance, restricting DC to a paltry total, which they then chased down with significant ease.

## Key Developments
*   **DC's Batting Collapse:** Delhi Capitals experienced a dramatic batting collapse, being bowled out for 75 runs in 16.3 overs. This included a tournament-record lowest Powerplay score of 13/6 and a concerning 8/6 at one point. Abishek Porel was the top scorer for DC with 30 runs.
*   **RCB's Dominant Bowling:** Josh Hazlewood (4/12) and Bhuvneshwar Kumar (3/5) were the chief architects of DC's downfall, collectively taking 7 wickets.
*   **Seamless Chase:** RCB chased the target of 76 in just 6.3 overs. Devdutt Padikkal remained unbeaten on 34 off 13 balls, supported by Virat Kohli's unbeaten 23 off 15 balls. Jacob Bethell also contributed a quickfire 20 off 11 balls at the start of the innings.
*   **Kohli's Milestone:** Virat Kohli made history during the chase, becoming the first batter to score 9,000 runs in IPL history.
*   **Record Victory:** The win was RCB's second-fastest in IPL history and one of their most dominant in terms of balls remaining.
